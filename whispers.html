It Speaks In Whispers

About

It Speaks In Whispers is a text based adventure game, following an adventurous soul seeking to explore the Whispering Woods, a place known to drive souls to madness. It focuses on a blend of humor and disturbing imagery as the player progresses further. Along the way they'll encounter several challenges and a few friendly faces. Will you learn the truth of what's blinding the minds of all who enter, or become another lost soul?
