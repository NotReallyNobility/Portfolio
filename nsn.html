About

The NSN website is a collection of my own comic creations, including settings, arcs, and of course, characters! Unfortunately the website is far from complete, with placeholder assets for the art due to the scale of it and the time constraints as I plan to make dedicated art specifically for the website. The website will focus on early characters for the time being as I'd rather not give away to many spoilers, though a sneak peek here or there shouldn't hurt. The website was designed with the Bootstrap and jQuery frameworks.
